Our Published Applications

SIA Software Innovations proudly presents our published Android applications on Google Play Store. We build purposeful, practical, and user-focused apps designed to solve everyday problems and enhance productivity.

SWIFT RECORDS

SWIFT RECORDS is a user-friendly offline accounting simulator for students. Designed for practical learning, it enables managing companies, ledgers, vouchers, stock items, and generating financial reports like Trial Balance, Profit & Loss, and Balance Sheet. It closely mirrors Tally’s workflow, but is optimized for classroom and home practice — no internet or login needed.

  • ✔ Offline ledger and voucher management
  • ✔ Stock adjustments and inventory tracking
  • ✔ Academic tool for students and educators
  • ✔ Daybook, Balance Sheet, and P&L generation
View on Google Play Store

SIP REFINE

SIP REFINE is an intuitive SIP calculator with corpus visualization, designed for investors and planners. It helps users model multiple SIPs with different start/end ages, monthly amounts, and return rates. With optional SWP (withdrawal) modeling and interactive charts, it provides a clear projection of your financial goals — all calculations are done locally for privacy.

  • ✔ Multi-SIP modeling with custom duration & return
  • ✔ Optional SWP (withdrawal) planner
  • ✔ Charts for overall corpus and individual SIPs
  • ✔ No data collection, 100% local and secure
